1. Motivation


Design plays an essential role in communicating between objects and users. Any device should be intuitive and easy to use. That being said, some give the user tough time to figure out how they work. In this project, we will look into a microwave, an ubiquitous device we see everyday, to see its current design and how we can make it becomes more user-friendly.


I bought a microwave from Amazon. It's a COMFEE's countertop microwave, first released in 2018. Here is a photo of the microwave, with the control interface on the right.
